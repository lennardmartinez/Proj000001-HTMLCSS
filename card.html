Reef Manta Ray

Reef Manta Rays have the largest brain to body weight ratio of any living fish

  • Scientific Name: Mobula alfredi
  • Average Disk Size: 3 to 3.5 m (11 ft)
  • Life Span: Mantas may live to at least 50 years old
  • Habitat: Tropical and subtropical parts of the Indo-Pacific.

A fully grown oceanic manta ray can reach a wing span of up to 7 metres and weigh up to two tonnes. That's as much as a white rhino.
